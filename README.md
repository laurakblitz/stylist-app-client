StyList is an application to help get you organized. 

Simply upload a photo of a piece of clothing from your wardrobe, select the category, and add it to your digital closet. 

Finally, the ability to sort through the pieces in your closet with the touch of a button!

With StyList, you can create a user with the ability to add, update and delete a post from your digital closet. 

StyList also allows you to create a wishlist of items you hope to one day have in your personal wardrboe. The wishlist items can also be updated and deleted.
